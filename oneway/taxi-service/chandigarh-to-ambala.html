Seamless Travel on NH-44

The Chandigarh–Ambala corridor is one of North India's most well-connected stretches. Running along National Highway 44, the 48 km journey takes roughly 45 minutes under normal traffic conditions — making it ideal for business trips, airport connections, or inter-city commutes. KDM Cab Service operates this route around the clock with a fleet of maintained, GPS-tracked vehicles.

One Way Cab — Simple & Affordable

With our one-way cab option, you only pay for the distance you travel. No return charges, no round-trip pricing tricks. Whether you're travelling solo in a Dzire or moving a group in an Innova Crysta, the fare is fixed and shown upfront before you book.
