Chandigarh to Amritsar Cab Service

KDM Cab Service offers reliable and comfortable one-way cab service from Chandigarh to Amritsar. Our experienced drivers and well-maintained vehicles ensure a smooth journey. For exact distance and travel time, please use the map below or contact us.

About the Route

The Chandigarh to Amritsar route is one of the most traveled corridors in Punjab, connecting the tri-city region to the holy city of the Golden Temple. The highway passes through Ludhiana and Jalandhar, both excellent stops for refreshments. Our drivers are experienced on this route in all weather and traffic conditions.

Things to Do in Amritsar

Amritsar is home to the iconic Sri Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple), Jallianwala Bagh, Wagah Border ceremony, and the vibrant Katra Jaimal Singh market. Whether you are visiting for pilgrimage, tourism, or business, our cabs ensure you arrive comfortably and on time.
